About Energy
Trucking

Our team has been involved in the industry since 2014, driven by ambition and curiosity to excel and redefine freight transport operations through innovative solutions.

Starting with just five people in a small Orlando office, we have grown to over 200 employees and expanded to three offices in the past five years.

Our ability to navigate various market conditions can be attributed to the collaboration of our expert team and the implementation of novel strategies developed through years of industry experience

Our Values

1

Safety

At our trucking company, safety is ingrained in our core values. We place a strong emphasis on the well-being of our employees, drivers, and the communities we serve. By prioritizing safety, we create a secure environment, minimize accidents, and deliver reliable transportation solutions to our valued clients.

2

Efficiency

By streamlining processes, maximizing resources, and minimizing waste, we ensure timely deliveries, cost-effectiveness, and exceptional service for our clients.

Innovation

By embracing new technologies, ideas, and solutions, we strive to constantly improve our services, enhance operational efficiency, and stay ahead in a rapidly changing industry.

Our main office is in Chicago, Illinois - but we also have drop yards strategically located in Philadelphia, Columbus, Saint Louis, Nashville, Dallas, Ontario, Seattle, and Orlando.
